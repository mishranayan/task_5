Delicious Food of Sitamarhi

Traditional Cuisine

Sitamarhi, located in Bihar, offers a variety of traditional Bihari food. The staple food includes rice, dal (lentils), roti, and seasonal vegetables. Dishes are often flavored with mustard oil and local spices.

Popular Dishes

Some well-known dishes from the region include:

  • Litti Chokha: Roasted wheat balls stuffed with sattu, served with mashed potatoes and brinjal.
  • Khichdi: A simple mix of rice and lentils, often eaten with ghee, pickle, and papad.
  • Thekua: A sweet snack made with wheat flour and jaggery, especially during Chhath Puja.

Festive Foods

During festivals like Chhath, Holi, and Diwali, homes in Sitamarhi are filled with the aroma of special sweets and snacks such as gujiya, malpua, and dahi vada.
