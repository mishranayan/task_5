Top Places to Visit in Sitamarhi

Sitamarhi, a town of historical and religious importance in Bihar, is believed to be the birthplace of Goddess Sita. It is filled with sacred sites, cultural heritage, and peaceful natural spots that attract pilgrims and tourists alike.

Must-Visit Places

  • Janaki Mandir (Sitamarhi): This temple is dedicated to Goddess Sita and is considered her birthplace. A major pilgrimage site for Hindus.
  • Punaura Dham: Located about 5 km west of Sitamarhi town, it is another important religious site associated with the life of Sita.
  • Haleshwar Sthan: An ancient Shiva temple believed to have been established by King Videha.
  • Baghi Math: A peaceful math (monastery) and ashram ideal for spiritual reflection and calmness.

Nearby Attractions

You can also explore nearby towns such as Darbhanga and Muzaffarpur for their historical palaces, temples, and cultural experiences.
